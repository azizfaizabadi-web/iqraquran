Islamic Studies

Comprehensive online Islamic education for kids and adults, connecting you to the basics of Islam.

About Islamic Studies

Islamic Studies refers to studying Islam in-depth. All Islamic knowledge and courses are categorized as Islamic Studies. It covers many branches, ranging from Islamic History to the Biography of the Prophet Muhammad (S.A.W). Moreover, issues like Marriage, Divorce, and Relationships are thoroughly discussed in the light of the Quran.

The basics of Islam, the pillars of Islam, the right way to offer Namaz, Hajj, the importance of Zakat, and so on are also elaborated in Islamic studies. It endeavors to instill all the goodness into their heart & soul and helps them to succeed in this life and the hereafter as well.

Benefits of Learning

  • Learn the correct way to pray and worship Allah (SWT)
  • Instill love and affection for Allah
  • Learn Tafseer of Surahs through engaging materials
  • Apply Islamic rules (studied in Fiqh) in daily life
  • Develop a love for Prophets and their teachings

What You'll Learn

1. The Five Pillars of Islam

Shahadah (Tawhid), Salah (Prayer), Zakah (Obligatory Charity), Sawm (Fasting Ramadan), Hajj (Pilgrimage).

2. Islamic Manners and Ethics

Sunnan and Adab of eating, drinking, sleeping. General Islamic Morals and Ethics (Piety, Truthfulness, Brotherhood). Islamic views on seeking knowledge and respecting elders.

3. Aqedah (Islamic Creed)

Foundations of faith and core Islamic creed principles.

4. Seerah and Islamic History

Biography of Prophet Muhammad (PBUH), stories of previous Prophets, and prominent Muslim figures throughout history.
